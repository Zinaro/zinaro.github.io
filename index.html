The Self-Taught Journey I use Arch, btw.

Zinar isn't just a user; he is root. A digital minimalist who despises bloatware and telemetry.

While others click buttons, I edit config files. I find peace in the terminal and power in open source. My workflow is keyboard-driven, my kernel is custom-compiled, and my editor is Neovim. Efficiency isn't just a goal; it's the only way to operate.

I didn't need a university degree to find my passion. I taught myself everything I know about frontend development, driven by pure curiosity and a love for creating things.

I specialize in building clean, functional, and visually appealing web applications. I understand the value of hard work and continuous learning. If you need someone who is eager to build, adapt, and deliver, I'm your developer.
